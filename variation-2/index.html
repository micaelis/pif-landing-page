How it works

Three steps, and the library is yours

No fees, no haggling. Just readers keeping good books in motion.

1

Share a book

Pick a book you've finished and loved, list it on your shelf, and pop it in the mail to another member.

2

Earn a credit

Every book you send forward earns you one credit — your ticket to the whole community library.

3

Request any book

Spend your credit on any title in the library. It's mailed to your door by the reader who shared it.
